Invicta Reserve 50mm Jason Taylor Excursion Bracelet Watch w/ Three-Slot Dive Case

Choices: Red or Yellow dial accent

Ingenious architecture and design blends with formidable capabilities on this Invicta Reserve Jason Taylor Excursion!

Take a stylish step forward with the round black stainless steel case. Measuring 50mm wide the case presents its imposing stature with two black pedal-style function pushers on either side of a texture edged screw down crown. A black bezel tops the case with Arabic numerals etched at five minute increments and four white arrow accents at the cardinal points. “Limited Edition” appears on the case back with your number out of 999 while “JT” caps the crown. This Excursion expertly executes the time on a Swiss Made Ronda 5040.D Quartz Chronograph movement.

A Flame Fusion crystal hovers above the round black carbon fiber dial and its standout accents in red or yellow. A 60-seconds subdial sits near 2:00 a date window finds its place near 4:00 a 1/10th-seconds subdial resides above 6:00 and a 30-minute subdial lands near 10:00. The red accented dial features round luminous Tritnite index markers with red trim at all hour positions except 12:00 Tritnite and red trim hour and minute hands and solid red subdial and chronograph hands. The yellow accented dial boasts Tritnite index markers and hour and minute hands with yellow trim and solid yellow subdial and chronograph hands. “JT” and “Invicta Reserve” share the space below 12:00 for the final detail.

Cool black links come together for the stainless steel bracelet. The three link design secures with a deployant clasp and a safety catch etched “JT”. A timepiece uniquely styled with you in mind make this Invicta Reserve Jason Taylor Excursion the newest addition to your timepiece collection today!
